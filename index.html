Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yong Li and Prof. Fengli Xu. I'm interested in LLM Reasoning, LLM Agent, and AI Scientist.

Previously, I received my B.E. degree from the School of Electronics and Information Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) in June 2024, ranking 1/227 with a GPA of 4.0/4.0.

My research focuses on LLM Reasoning and LLM Agent, with particular interest in LLM reasoning optimization, Self-evolving Agent, and improving Agent capabilities in embodied AI, gaming, web, recommendation, and scientific research domains.

Research Experience

  • AgentSquare: Automatic LLM Agent Search in Modular Design Space
    Tsinghua FIBLab | ICLR 2025

    Designed a modular design space with four standardized modules (planning, reasoning, tool use, and memory) and an automated agent architecture search framework via module evolution and recombination.

    Results: Achieved 17.2% average performance improvement over best human-designed agents across 6 cross-domain benchmarks.

Internship Experience

  • Research Intern, Noah's Ark Lab
    Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. | May 2025 – August 2025

    Proposed a value-guided hierarchical agent search framework (AgentSwift) with hierarchical search space, value model training via balanced Bayesian sampling, and uncertainty-based hierarchical Monte Carlo tree search.

    Results: Achieved 8.34% improvement over best human-designed agents across 7 benchmarks. Deployed in Huawei smartphone agent scenarios with 21.6% improvement on SPA-bench.
